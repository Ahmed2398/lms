EDMS Services

What is EDMS?

  • A document management solution (DMS) is a solution used to track, manage, store and retrieve documents.
  • Beginning in the 1980s, a number of vendors began developing software systems to manage paper-based documents. These systems dealt with paper documents, which included not only printed and published documents, but also photographs, prints, etc. Later developers began to write a second type of system which could manage electronic documents (Wikipedia).
  • This Software is needed to be supported by Operational Teams in order to convert scanned pages into retrievable documents through the Indexing and Validation processes.

Why does it matter?

  • Security: Documents in any organization are regarded as a reference to financial and non financial data of strategic importance.
  • Legal: in many countries the physical documents are the reference in any legal dispute, hence the need for a strong solution that blends the document digitization with the ability to retrieve physical documents.
  • Cost reduction: the cost of managing digitized documents is significantly lower than physical documents
  • Planning: digitizing organizations’ documents allow for easier access to historical information for efficient planning.
